Every Drop Counts

Your voluntary donation is vital. If an accident, surgery, or treatment occurs, LifeLink allows hospitals and individuals to source blood within minutes. Registering as a donor takes under 3 minutes.

Who Can Donate?

  • Age between 18 and 65 years old.
  • Weight is at least 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • Has not donated blood in the last 56 days.
  • In good general health, feeling well.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to basic questions regarding blood donations.

How much blood is collected?

Usually, 350ml to 450ml (one unit) of blood is collected. Your body recovers the fluid volume within 24 to 48 hours, and replenishes red blood cells in 4-6 weeks.

Is it safe to donate blood?

Yes. LifeLink only links users to sterile hospital labs. All extraction tools are disposable, single-use, and opened only for you, making the process 100% sterile.

How long does the donation process take?

The actual blood extraction takes only 8-10 minutes. However, the registration, medical pre-screening, and resting refresheners require about 45 to 60 minutes in total.
